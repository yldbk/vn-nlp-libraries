BO_LUAT_HINH_SU
CUA_NUOC_CONG_HOA_XA_HOI_CHU_NGHIA_VIET_NAM_SO 15/1999/QH10
LOI_NOI_DAU
Phap_luat hinh_su la mot trong nhung cong_cu sac_ben , huu_hieu de dau_tranh phong_ngua va chong toi_pham , gop_phan dac_luc vao viec bao_ve doc_lap , chu_quyen , thong_nhat va toan_ven lanh_tho cua To_quoc Viet_Nam xa_hoi_chu_nghia , bao_ve loi_ich cua Nha_nuoc , quyen , loi_ich hop_phap cua cong_dan , to_chuc , gop_phan duy_tri trat_tu an_toan xa_hoi , trat_tu quan_ly kinh_te , bao_dam cho moi nguoi duoc song trong mot moi_truong xa_hoi va sinh_thai an_toan , lanh_manh , mang tinh_nhan van cao . Dong_thoi , phap_luat hinh_su gop_phan tich_cuc loai_bo nhung yeu_to gay_can tro cho tien_trinh doi_moi va su_nghiep cong_nghiep_hoa , hien_dai_hoa dat_nuoc vi muc_tieu dan giau , nuoc manh , xa_hoi cong_bang , van_minh .
Bo_luat hinh_su nay duoc xay_dung tren co_so ke_thua va phat_huy nhung nguyen_tac , che_dinh phap_luat hinh_su cua_nuoc ta , nhat_la cua Bo_luat hinh_su nam 1985 , cung nhu nhung bai_hoc kinh_nghiem tu thuc_tien dau_tranh phong_ngua va chong toi_pham trong nhieu thap_ky qua cua qua_trinh xay_dung va bao_ve To_quoc .
Bo_luat hinh_su the_hien tinh_than chu_dong phong_ngua va kien_quyet dau_tranh chong toi_pham va thong_qua hinh_phat de ran_de , giao_duc , cam_hoa , cai_tao nguoi pham_toi tro_thanh nguoi luong_thien ; qua_do , boi_duong cho moi cong_dan tinh_than , y_thuc lam_chu xa_hoi , y_thuc tuan_thu phap_luat , chu_dong tham_gia phong_ngua va chong toi_pham .
Thi_hanh nghiem_chinh Bo_luat hinh_su la nhiem_vu chung cua tat_ca cac co_quan , to_chuc va toan_the nhan_dan .
PHAN_CHUNG
Chuong I
DIEU_KHOAN_CO_BAN
Dieu 1 . Nhiem_vu cua Bo_luat hinh_su
Bo_luat hinh su_co nhiem_vu bao_ve che_do xa_hoi_chu_nghia , quyen lam_chu cua nhan_dan , bao_ve quyen binh_dang giua dong_bao cac dan_toc , bao_ve loi_ich cua Nha_nuoc , quyen , loi_ich hop_phap cua cong_dan , to_chuc , bao_ve trat_tu phap_luat xa_hoi_chu_nghia , chong moi hanh_vi pham_toi ; dong_thoi giao_duc moi nguoi y_thuc tuan theo phap_luat , dau_tranh phong_ngua va chong toi_pham .
De thuc_hien nhiem_vu do , Bo_luat quy_dinh toi_pham va hinh_phat doi_voi nguoi pham_toi .
Dieu 2 . Co_so cua trach_nhiem hinh_su
Chi nguoi nao pham mot toi_da duoc Bo_luat hinh_su quy_dinh moi_phai chiu trach_nhiem hinh_su .
Dieu 3 . Nguyen_tac xu_ly
1 . Moi hanh_vi pham_toi phai duoc phat_hien kip_thoi , xu_ly nhanh_chong , cong_minh theo dung phap_luat .
2 . Moi nguoi pham_toi deu binh_dang truoc phap_luat , khong_phan biet nam , nu , dan_toc , tin_nguong , ton_giao , thanh_phan , dia_vi xa_hoi .
Nghiem_tri nguoi chu_muu , cam_dau , chi_huy , ngoan_co chong_doi , luu_manh , con_do , tai_pham nguy_hiem , loi_dung chuc_vu , quyen_han de pham_toi ; nguoi pham_toi dung thu_doan xao_quyet , co to_chuc , co_tinh chat chuyen_nghiep , co_y gay hau_qua nghiem_trong .
Khoan_hong doi_voi nguoi tu_thu , thanh_khan khai_bao , to_giac nguoi dong_pham , lap_cong chuoc toi , an_nan hoi_cai , tu_nguyen sua_chua hoac boi_thuong thiet_hai gay ra .
3 . Doi_voi nguoi lan dau pham_toi it nghiem_trong , da_hoi cai , thi co_the ap_dung hinh_phat nhe hon hinh_phat tu , giao ho cho co_quan , to_chuc hoac gia_dinh giam_sat , giao_duc .
4 . Doi_voi nguoi bi phat tu_thi buoc ho phai chap_hanh hinh_phat trong trai_giam , phai lao_dong , hoc tap_de tro_thanh nguoi co_ich cho xa_hoi ; neu ho co nhieu tien_bo thi xet de giam viec chap_hanh hinh_phat .
5 . Nguoi da chap_hanh xong hinh_phat duoc tao dieu_kien lam_an , sinh_song luong_thien , hoa_nhap voi cong_dong , khi co du dieu_kien do luat_dinh thi duoc xoa an tich .
Dieu 4 . Trach_nhiem dau_tranh phong_ngua va chong toi_pham
1 . Cac co_quan Cong_an , Kiem_sat , Toa_an , Tu_phap , Thanh_tra va cac co_quan huu_quan khac co trach_nhiem thi_hanh day_du chuc_nang , nhiem_vu cua_minh , dong_thoi huong_dan , giup_do cac co_quan khac cua Nha_nuoc , to_chuc , cong_dan dau_tranh phong_ngua va chong toi_pham , giam_sat va giao_duc nguoi pham_toi tai_cong dong .
2 . Cac co_quan , to_chuc co nhiem_vu giao_duc nhung nguoi thuoc quyen quan_ly cua_minh nang cao canh_giac , y_thuc bao_ve phap_luat va tuan theo phap_luat , ton_trong cac quy_tac cua cuoc_song xa_hoi_chu_nghia ; kip_thoi co bien_phap loai_tru nguyen_nhan va dieu_kien gay ra toi_pham trong co_quan , to_chuc cua_minh .
3 . Moi cong_dan co_nghia vu tich_cuc tham_gia dau_tranh phong_ngua va chong toi_pham .
Chuong II
HIEU_LUC_CUA_BO_LUAT_HINH_SU
Dieu 5 . Hieu_luc cua Bo_luat hinh_su doi_voi nhung hanh_vi pham_toi tren lanh_tho nuoc Cong_hoa xa_hoi_chu_nghia Viet_Nam
1 . Bo_luat hinh_su duoc ap_dung doi_voi moi hanh_vi pham_toi thuc_hien tren lanh_tho nuoc Cong_hoa xa_hoi_chu_nghia Viet_Nam .
2 . Doi_voi nguoi nuoc_ngoai pham_toi tren lanh_tho nuoc Cong_hoa xa_hoi_chu_nghia Viet_Nam thuoc doi_tuong duoc huong cac quyen mien_tru ngoai_giao hoac quyen uu_dai va mien_tru ve lanh_su theo phap_luat Viet_Nam , theo cac dieu_uoc quoc_te ma nuoc Cong_hoa xa_hoi_chu_nghia Viet_Nam ky_ket hoac tham_gia hoac theo tap_quan quoc_te , thi van_de trach_nhiem hinh_su cua ho duoc giai_quyet bang con duong ngoai_giao .
Dieu 6 . Hieu_luc cua Bo_luat hinh_su doi_voi nhung hanh_vi pham_toi o ngoai lanh_tho nuoc Cong_hoa xa_hoi_chu_nghia Viet_Nam
1 . Cong_dan Viet_Nam pham_toi o ngoai lanh_tho nuoc Cong_hoa xa_hoi_chu_nghia Viet_Nam co_the bi truy_cuu trach_nhiem hinh_su tai Viet_Nam theo Bo_luat nay .
Quy_dinh nay cung duoc ap_dung doi_voi nguoi khong quoc_tich thuong_tru o nuoc Cong_hoa xa_hoi_chu_nghia Viet_Nam .
2 . Nguoi nuoc_ngoai pham_toi o ngoai lanh_tho nuoc Cong_hoa xa_hoi_chu_nghia Viet_Nam co_the bi truy_cuu trach_nhiem hinh_su theo Bo_luat hinh_su Viet_Nam trong nhung truong_hop duoc quy_dinh trong cac dieu_uoc quoc_te ma nuoc Cong_hoa xa_hoi_chu_nghia Viet_Nam ky_ket hoac tham_gia .
Dieu 7 . Hieu_luc cua Bo_luat hinh_su ve thoi_gian
1 . Dieu_luat duoc ap_dung doi_voi mot hanh_vi pham_toi la dieu_luat dang co_hieu luc thi_hanh tai thoi_diem ma hanh_vi pham_toi duoc thuc_hien .
2 . Dieu_luat quy_dinh mot toi_pham moi , mot hinh_phat nang hon , mot tinh_tiet tang nang moi hoac han_che pham_vi ap_dung an_treo , mien trach_nhiem hinh_su , mien hinh_phat , giam hinh_phat , xoa an tich va cac quy_dinh khac khong co loi cho_nguoi pham_toi , thi khong duoc ap_dung doi_voi hanh vi_pham toi_da thuc_hien truoc khi dieu_luat do co_hieu luc thi_hanh .
3 . Dieu_luat xoa_bo mot toi_pham , mot hinh_phat , mot tinh_tiet tang nang , quy_dinh mot hinh_phat nhe hon , mot tinh_tiet giam nhe moi hoac mo_rong pham_vi ap_dung an_treo , mien trach_nhiem hinh_su , mien hinh_phat , giam hinh_phat , xoa an tich va cac quy_dinh khac co loi cho_nguoi pham_toi , thi duoc ap_dung doi_voi hanh vi_pham toi_da thuc_hien truoc khi dieu_luat do co_hieu luc thi_hanh .
Chuong III
TOI_PHAM
Dieu 8 . Khai_niem toi_pham
1 . Toi_pham la hanh_vi nguy_hiem cho xa_hoi duoc quy_dinh trong Bo_luat hinh_su , do_nguoi co_nang luc trach_nhiem hinh_su thuc_hien mot_cach co_y hoac vo_y , xam_pham doc_lap , chu_quyen , thong_nhat , toan_ven lanh_tho To_quoc , xam_pham che_do chinh_tri , che_do kinh_te , nen van_hoa , quoc_phong , an_ninh , trat_tu , an_toan xa_hoi , quyen , loi_ich hop_phap cua to_chuc , xam_pham tinh_mang , suc_khoe , danh_du , nhan_pham , tu_do , tai_san , cac quyen , loi_ich hop_phap khac cua cong_dan , xam_pham nhung linh_vuc khac cua trat_tu phap_luat xa_hoi_chu_nghia .
2 . Can_cu vao tinh_chat va muc_do nguy_hiem cho xa_hoi cua hanh_vi duoc quy_dinh trong Bo_luat nay , toi_pham duoc phan thanh toi_pham it nghiem_trong , toi_pham nghiem_trong , toi_pham rat nghiem_trong va toi_pham dac_biet nghiem_trong .
3 . Toi_pham it nghiem_trong la toi_pham gay nguy_hai khong lon cho xa_hoi ma muc cao nhat cua khung_hinh_phat doi_voi toi ay la den ba nam tu ; toi_pham nghiem_trong la toi_pham gay nguy_hai lon cho xa_hoi ma muc cao nhat cua khung_hinh_phat doi_voi toi ay la den bay nam tu ; toi_pham rat nghiem_trong la toi_pham gay nguy_hai rat lon cho xa_hoi ma muc cao nhat cua khung_hinh_phat doi_voi toi ay la den muoi lam nam tu ; toi_pham dac_biet nghiem_trong la toi_pham gay nguy_hai dac_biet lon cho xa_hoi ma muc cao nhat cua khung_hinh_phat doi_voi toi ay la tren muoi lam nam tu , tu chung_than hoac tu_hinh .
4 . Nhung hanh_vi tuy_co dau_hieu cua toi_pham , nhung tinh_chat nguy_hiem cho xa_hoi khong dang_ke , thi khong phai la toi_pham va duoc xu_ly bang cac bien_phap khac .
Dieu 9 . Co_y pham_toi
Co_y pham_toi la pham_toi trong nhung truong_hop sau day :
1 . Nguoi pham_toi nhan_thuc ro hanh_vi cua_minh la nguy_hiem cho xa_hoi , thay truoc hau_qua cua hanh_vi do va mong_muon hau_qua xay ra ;
2 . Nguoi pham_toi nhan_thuc ro hanh_vi cua_minh la nguy_hiem cho xa_hoi , thay truoc hau_qua cua hanh_vi do co_the xay ra , tuy khong mong_muon nhung van co y_thuc de mac cho hau_qua xay ra .
Dieu 10 . Vo_y pham_toi
Vo_y pham_toi la pham_toi trong nhung truong_hop sau day :
1 . Nguoi pham_toi tuy thay truoc hanh_vi cua_minh co_the gay ra hau_qua nguy_hai cho xa_hoi , nhung cho rang hau_qua do se khong xay ra hoac co_the ngan_ngua duoc ;
2 . Nguoi pham_toi khong thay truoc hanh_vi cua_minh co_the gay ra hau_qua nguy_hai cho xa_hoi , mac_du phai thay truoc va co_the thay truoc hau_qua do .
Dieu 11 . Su_kien bat_ngo
Nguoi thuc_hien hanh_vi gay hau_qua nguy_hai cho xa_hoi do su_kien bat_ngo , tuc_la trong truong_hop khong_the thay truoc hoac khong buoc phai thay truoc hau_qua cua hanh_vi do , thi khong phai chiu trach_nhiem hinh_su .
Dieu 12 . Tuoi chiu trach_nhiem hinh_su
1 . Nguoi tu du 16 tuoi tro len phai chiu trach_nhiem hinh_su ve moi toi_pham .
2 . Nguoi tu du 14 tuoi tro len , nhung chua du 16 tuoi phai chiu trach_nhiem hinh_su ve toi_pham rat nghiem_trong do co_y hoac toi_pham dac_biet nghiem_trong .
Dieu 13 . Tinh_trang khong co_nang luc trach_nhiem hinh_su
1 . Nguoi thuc_hien hanh_vi nguy_hiem cho xa_hoi trong khi dang mac benh_tam_than hoac mot benh khac lam mat kha_nang nhan_thuc hoac kha_nang dieu_khien hanh_vi cua_minh , thi khong phai chiu trach_nhiem hinh_su ; doi_voi nguoi nay , phai ap_dung bien_phap bat_buoc chua benh .
2 . Nguoi pham_toi trong khi co_nang luc trach_nhiem hinh_su , nhung da lam vao tinh_trang quy_dinh tai_khoan 1 Dieu_nay truoc khi bi ket_an , thi cung duoc ap_dung bien_phap bat_buoc chua benh . Sau khi khoi benh , nguoi do co_the phai chiu trach_nhiem hinh_su .
Dieu 14 . Pham_toi trong tinh_trang say do_dung ruou hoac chat kich_thich manh khac
Nguoi pham_toi trong tinh_trang say do_dung ruou hoac chat kich_thich manh khac , thi van phai chiu trach_nhiem hinh_su .
Dieu 15 . Phong_ve chinh_dang
1 . Phong_ve chinh_dang la hanh_vi cua nguoi vi bao_ve loi_ich cua Nha_nuoc , cua to_chuc , bao_ve quyen , loi_ich chinh_dang cua_minh hoac cua nguoi khac , ma chong_tra lai mot_cach can_thiet nguoi dang co hanh_vi xam_pham cac loi_ich noi tren .
Phong_ve chinh_dang khong phai la toi_pham .
2 . Vuot qua gioi_han phong_ve chinh_dang la hanh_vi chong_tra ro_rang qua muc can_thiet , khong phu_hop voi tinh_chat va muc_do nguy_hiem cho xa_hoi cua hanh_vi xam_hai .
Nguoi co hanh_vi vuot qua gioi_han phong_ve chinh_dang phai chiu trach_nhiem hinh_su .
Dieu 16 . Tinh_the cap_thiet
1 . Tinh_the cap_thiet la tinh_the cua nguoi vi muon tranh mot nguy_co dang thuc_te de_doa loi_ich cua Nha_nuoc , cua to_chuc , quyen , loi_ich chinh_dang cua_minh hoac cua nguoi khac ma khong con cach nao khac la phai gay mot thiet_hai nho hon thiet_hai can ngan_ngua .
Hanh_vi gay thiet_hai trong tinh_the cap_thiet khong phai la toi_pham .
2 . Trong truong_hop thiet_hai gay ra ro_rang vuot qua yeu_cau cua tinh_the cap_thiet , thi nguoi gay thiet_hai do phai chiu trach_nhiem hinh_su .
Dieu 17 . Chuan_bi pham_toi
Chuan_bi pham_toi la tim_kiem , sua_soan cong_cu , phuong_tien hoac tao ra nhung dieu_kien khac de thuc_hien toi_pham .
Nguoi chuan_bi pham mot toi rat nghiem_trong hoac mot toi dac_biet nghiem_trong , thi_phai chiu trach_nhiem hinh_su ve toi dinh thuc_hien .
Dieu 18 . Pham_toi chua_dat
Pham_toi chua_dat la co_y thuc_hien toi_pham nhung khong thuc_hien duoc den_cung vi nhung nguyen_nhan ngoai y_muon cua nguoi pham_toi .
Nguoi pham_toi chua_dat phai chiu trach_nhiem hinh_su ve toi_pham chua_dat .
Dieu 19 . Tu_y nua_chung cham_dut viec pham_toi
Tu_y nua_chung cham_dut viec pham_toi la tu minh khong thuc_hien toi_pham den_cung , tuy khong co gi ngan_can .
Nguoi tu_y nua_chung cham_dut viec pham_toi duoc mien trach_nhiem hinh_su ve toi dinh pham ; neu hanh_vi thuc_te da thuc_hien co du yeu_to cau_thanh cua mot toi khac , thi nguoi do phai chiu trach_nhiem hinh_su ve toi nay .
Dieu 20 . Dong_pham
1 . Dong_pham la truong_hop co hai nguoi tro len co_y cung thuc_hien mot toi_pham .
2 . Nguoi to_chuc , nguoi thuc_hanh , nguoi xui_giuc , nguoi giup_suc deu la nhung nguoi dong_pham .
Nguoi thuc_hanh la nguoi truc_tiep thuc_hien toi_pham .
Nguoi to_chuc la nguoi chu_muu , cam_dau , chi_huy viec thuc_hien toi_pham .
Nguoi xui_giuc la nguoi kich_dong , du_do , thuc_day nguoi khac thuc_hien toi_pham .
Nguoi giup_suc la nguoi tao nhung dieu_kien tinh_than hoac vat_chat cho viec thuc_hien toi_pham .
3 . Pham_toi co to_chuc la hinh_thuc dong_pham co_su cau_ket chat_che giua nhung nguoi cung thuc_hien toi_pham .
Dieu 21 . Che_giau toi_pham
Nguoi nao khong hua_hen truoc , nhung sau khi biet toi_pham duoc thuc_hien , da che_giau nguoi pham_toi , cac dau_vet , tang_vat cua toi_pham hoac co hanh_vi khac can_tro viec phat_hien , dieu_tra , xu_ly nguoi pham_toi , thi_phai chiu trach_nhiem hinh_su ve toi che_giau toi_pham trong nhung truong_hop ma Bo_luat nay quy_dinh .
Dieu 22 . Khong to_giac toi_pham
1 . Nguoi nao biet ro toi_pham dang duoc chuan_bi , dang duoc thuc_hien hoac da duoc thuc_hien ma khong to_giac , thi_phai chiu trach_nhiem hinh_su ve toi khong to_giac toi_pham trong nhung truong_hop quy_dinh tai Dieu 313 cua Bo_luat nay .
2 . Nguoi khong to_giac la ong , ba , cha , me , con , chau , anh_chi_em ruot , vo hoac chong cua nguoi pham_toi chi phai chiu trach_nhiem hinh_su trong truong_hop khong to_giac cac toi xam_pham an_ninh quoc_gia hoac cac toi khac la toi dac_biet nghiem_trong quy_dinh tai Dieu 313 cua Bo_luat nay .
Chuong IV
THOI_HIEU_TRUY_CUU_TRACH_NHIEM_HINH_SU . MIEN_TRACH_NHIEM_HINH_SU
Dieu 23 . Thoi_hieu truy_cuu trach_nhiem hinh_su
1 . Thoi_hieu truy_cuu trach_nhiem hinh_su la thoi_han do Bo_luat nay quy_dinh ma khi het thoi_han do_thi nguoi pham_toi khong bi truy_cuu trach_nhiem hinh_su .
2 . Thoi_hieu truy_cuu trach_nhiem hinh_su duoc quy_dinh nhu sau :
a ) Nam nam doi_voi cac toi_pham it nghiem_trong ;
b ) Muoi nam doi_voi cac toi_pham nghiem_trong ;
c ) Muoi lam nam doi_voi cac toi_pham rat nghiem_trong ;
d ) Hai muoi nam doi_voi cac toi_pham dac_biet nghiem_trong .
3 . Thoi_hieu truy_cuu trach_nhiem hinh_su duoc_tinh tu ngay toi_pham duoc thuc_hien . Neu trong thoi_han quy_dinh tai_khoan 2 Dieu_nay nguoi pham_toi lai pham_toi moi ma Bo_luat quy_dinh muc cao nhat cua khung_hinh_phat doi_voi toi ay tren mot nam tu , thi thoi_gian da qua khong duoc_tinh va thoi_hieu doi_voi toi cu duoc_tinh lai ke tu ngay pham_toi moi .
Neu trong thoi_han noi tren , nguoi pham_toi co_tinh tron_tranh va da_co lenh truy_na , thi thoi_gian tron_tranh khong duoc_tinh va thoi_hieu tinh lai ke tu khi nguoi do ra tu_thu hoac bi bat_giu .
Dieu 24 . Khong ap_dung thoi_hieu truy_cuu trach_nhiem hinh_su
Khong ap_dung thoi_hieu truy_cuu trach_nhiem hinh_su quy_dinh tai Dieu 23 cua Bo_luat nay doi_voi cac toi quy_dinh tai Chuong XI va Chuong XIV cua Bo_luat nay .
Dieu 25 . Mien trach_nhiem hinh_su
1 . Nguoi pham_toi duoc mien trach_nhiem hinh_su , neu khi tien_hanh dieu_tra , truy_to hoac xet_xu , do chuyen_bien cua tinh_hinh ma hanh_vi pham_toi hoac nguoi pham_toi khong con nguy_hiem cho xa_hoi nua .
2 . Trong truong_hop truoc khi hanh_vi pham_toi bi phat_giac , nguoi pham toi_da tu_thu , khai ro su_viec , gop_phan co hieu_qua vao viec phat_hien va dieu_tra toi_pham , co_gang han_che den muc thap nhat hau_qua cua toi_pham , thi cung co_the duoc mien trach_nhiem hinh_su .
3 . Nguoi pham_toi duoc mien trach_nhiem hinh_su khi co quyet_dinh dai_xa .
Chuong V
HINH_PHAT
Dieu 26 . Khai_niem hinh_phat
Hinh_phat la bien_phap cuong_che nghiem_khac nhat cua Nha_nuoc nham tuoc bo hoac han_che quyen , loi_ich cua nguoi pham_toi .
Hinh_phat duoc quy_dinh trong Bo_luat hinh_su va do Toa_an quyet_dinh .
Dieu 27 . Muc_dich cua hinh_phat
Hinh_phat khong_chi nham trung_tri nguoi pham_toi ma con giao_duc ho_tro thanh nguoi co_ich cho xa_hoi , co y_thuc tuan theo phap_luat va cac quy_tac cua cuoc_song xa_hoi_chu_nghia , ngan_ngua ho pham_toi moi . Hinh_phat con nham giao_duc nguoi khac ton_trong phap_luat , dau_tranh phong_ngua va chong toi_pham .
Dieu 28 . Cac hinh_phat
Hinh_phat bao_gom hinh_phat chinh va hinh_phat bo_sung .
1 . Hinh_phat chinh bao_gom :
a ) Canh_cao ;
b ) Phat tien ;
c ) Cai_tao khong giam_giu ;
d ) Truc_xuat ;
d ) Tu co thoi_han ;
e ) Tu chung_than ;
g ) Tu_hinh .
2 . Hinh_phat bo_sung bao_gom :
a ) Cam dam_nhiem chuc_vu , cam hanh_nghe hoac lam cong_viec nhat_dinh ;
b ) Cam_cu tru ;
c ) Quan che ;
d ) Tuoc mot_so quyen_cong_dan ;
d ) Tich_thu tai_san ;
e ) Phat tien , khi khong ap_dung la hinh_phat chinh ;
g ) Truc_xuat , khi khong ap_dung la hinh_phat chinh .
3 . Doi_voi moi toi_pham , nguoi pham_toi chi bi ap_dung mot hinh_phat chinh va co_the bi ap_dung mot hoac mot_so hinh_phat bo_sung .
Dieu 29 . Canh_cao
Canh_cao duoc ap_dung doi_voi nguoi pham_toi it nghiem_trong va co nhieu tinh_tiet giam nhe , nhung chua den muc mien hinh_phat .
Dieu 30 . Phat tien
1 . Phat tien duoc ap_dung la hinh_phat chinh doi_voi nguoi pham_toi it nghiem_trong xam_pham trat_tu quan_ly kinh_te , trat_tu cong_cong , trat_tu quan_ly hanh_chinh va mot_so toi_pham khac do Bo_luat nay quy_dinh .
2 . Phat tien duoc ap_dung la hinh_phat bo_sung doi_voi nguoi pham cac toi ve tham_nhung , ma_tuy hoac nhung toi_pham khac do Bo_luat nay quy_dinh .
3 . Muc phat tien duoc quyet_dinh tuy theo tinh_chat va muc_do nghiem_trong cua toi_pham duoc thuc_hien , dong_thoi co xet den tinh_hinh tai_san cua nguoi pham_toi , su_bien dong gia_ca , nhung khong duoc thap hon mot trieu dong .
4 . Tien phat co_the duoc nop mot lan hoac nhieu lan trong thoi_han do Toa_an quyet_dinh trong_ban an .
Dieu 31 . Cai_tao khong giam_giu
1 . Cai_tao khong giam_giu duoc ap_dung tu sau thang den ba nam doi_voi nguoi pham_toi it nghiem_trong hoac pham_toi nghiem_trong do Bo_luat nay quy_dinh ma dang co noi lam_viec on_dinh hoac co noi thuong_tru ro_rang , neu xet thay khong can_thiet phai cach_ly nguoi pham_toi khoi xa_hoi .
Neu nguoi bi ket_an da bi tam giu , tam giam_thi thoi_gian tam giu , tam giam duoc tru vao thoi_gian chap_hanh hinh_phat cai_tao khong giam_giu , cu mot ngay tam giu , tam giam bang ba ngay cai_tao khong giam_giu .
2 . Toa_an giao nguoi bi phat cai_tao khong giam_giu cho co_quan , to_chuc noi nguoi do lam_viec hoac chinh_quyen dia_phuong noi nguoi do thuong_tru de giam_sat , giao_duc . Gia_dinh nguoi bi ket_an co trach_nhiem phoi_hop voi co_quan , to_chuc , chinh_quyen dia_phuong trong viec giam_sat , giao_duc nguoi do .
3 . Nguoi bi ket_an phai thuc_hien mot_so nghia_vu theo cac quy_dinh ve cai_tao khong giam_giu va bi khau_tru mot phan thu_nhap tu 5% den 20% de sung quy nha_nuoc . Trong truong_hop dac_biet , Toa_an co_the cho mien viec khau_tru thu_nhap , nhung phai ghi ro ly_do trong_ban an .
Dieu 32 . Truc_xuat
Truc_xuat la buoc nguoi nuoc_ngoai bi ket_an phai roi khoi lanh_tho nuoc Cong_hoa xa_hoi_chu_nghia Viet_Nam .
Truc_xuat duoc Toa_an ap_dung la hinh_phat chinh hoac hinh_phat bo_sung trong tung truong_hop cu_the .
Dieu 33 . Tu co thoi_han
Tu co thoi_han la viec buoc nguoi bi ket_an phai chap_hanh hinh phat_tai trai_giam trong mot thoi_han nhat_dinh . Tu co thoi_han doi_voi nguoi pham mot toi co muc toi_thieu la ba_thang , muc toi_da la hai muoi nam .
Thoi_gian tam giu , tam giam duoc tru vao thoi_han chap_hanh hinh_phat tu , cu mot ngay tam giu , tam giam bang mot ngay tu .
Dieu 34 . Tu chung_than
Tu chung_than la hinh_phat tu khong thoi_han duoc ap_dung doi_voi nguoi pham_toi dac_biet nghiem_trong , nhung chua den muc bi xu_phat tu_hinh .
Khong ap_dung tu chung_than doi_voi nguoi chua thanh_nien pham_toi .
Dieu 35 . Tu_hinh
Tu_hinh la hinh_phat dac_biet chi ap_dung doi_voi nguoi pham_toi dac_biet nghiem_trong .
Khong ap_dung hinh_phat tu_hinh doi_voi nguoi chua thanh_nien pham_toi , doi_voi phu_nu co_thai hoac phu_nu dang nuoi con duoi 36 thang tuoi khi pham_toi hoac khi bi xet_xu .
Khong thi_hanh an tu_hinh doi_voi phu_nu co_thai , phu_nu dang nuoi con duoi 36 thang tuoi . Trong truong_hop nay hinh_phat tu_hinh chuyen thanh_tu chung_than .
Trong truong_hop nguoi bi ket_an tu_hinh duoc an giam , thi hinh_phat tu_hinh chuyen thanh_tu chung_than .
Dieu 36 . Cam dam_nhiem chuc_vu , cam hanh_nghe hoac lam cong_viec nhat_dinh
Cam dam_nhiem chuc_vu , cam hanh_nghe hoac lam cong_viec nhat_dinh duoc ap_dung khi xet thay neu de nguoi bi ket_an dam_nhiem chuc_vu , hanh_nghe hoac lam cong_viec do , thi co_the gay nguy_hai cho xa_hoi .
Thoi_han cam la tu mot nam den nam nam , ke tu ngay chap_hanh xong hinh_phat tu hoac tu ngay ban_an co_hieu luc phap_luat neu hinh_phat chinh la canh_cao , phat tien , cai_tao khong giam_giu hoac trong truong_hop nguoi bi ket_an duoc huong an_treo .
Dieu 37 . Cam_cu tru
Cam_cu tru la buoc nguoi bi ket_an phat tu khong duoc tam_tru va thuong_tru o mot_so dia_phuong nhat_dinh .
Thoi_han cam cu_tru la tu mot nam den nam nam , ke tu ngay chap_hanh xong hinh_phat tu .
Dieu 38 . Quan che
Quan che_la buoc nguoi bi ket_an phat tu phai cu_tru , lam_an sinh_song va cai_tao o mot dia_phuong nhat_dinh , co_su kiem_soat , giao_duc cua chinh_quyen va nhan_dan dia_phuong . Trong thoi_gian quan che , nguoi bi ket_an khong duoc tu_y ra khoi noi cu_tru , bi tuoc mot_so quyen_cong_dan theo Dieu 39 cua Bo_luat nay va bi cam hanh_nghe hoac lam cong_viec nhat_dinh .
Quan che duoc ap_dung doi_voi nguoi pham_toi xam_pham an_ninh quoc_gia , nguoi tai_pham nguy_hiem hoac trong nhung truong_hop khac do Bo_luat nay quy_dinh .
Thoi_han quan che_la tu mot nam den nam nam , ke tu ngay chap_hanh xong hinh_phat tu .
Dieu 39 . Tuoc mot_so quyen_cong_dan
1 . Cong_dan Viet_Nam bi ket_an phat tu_ve toi xam_pham an_ninh quoc_gia hoac toi_pham khac trong nhung truong_hop do Bo_luat nay quy_dinh , thi bi tuoc mot hoac mot_so quyen_cong_dan sau day :
a ) Quyen ung_cu , quyen bau_cu dai_bieu co_quan quyen_luc nha_nuoc ;
b ) Quyen lam_viec trong cac_co quan_nha nuoc va quyen phuc_vu trong luc_luong_vu_trang nhan_dan .
2 . Thoi_han tuoc mot_so quyen_cong_dan la tu mot nam den nam nam , ke tu ngay chap_hanh xong hinh_phat tu hoac ke tu ngay ban_an co_hieu luc phap_luat trong truong_hop nguoi bi ket_an duoc huong an_treo .
Dieu 40 . Tich_thu tai_san
Tich_thu tai_san la tuoc mot phan hoac toan_bo tai_san thuoc so_huu cua nguoi bi ket_an sung quy nha_nuoc . Tich_thu tai_san chi duoc ap_dung doi_voi nguoi bi ket_an ve toi nghiem_trong , toi rat nghiem_trong hoac toi dac_biet nghiem_trong trong truong_hop do Bo_luat nay quy_dinh .
Khi tich_thu toan_bo tai_san van_de cho_nguoi bi ket_an va gia_dinh ho co dieu_kien sinh_song .
Chuong VI
CAC_BIEN_PHAP_TU_PHAP
Dieu 41 . Tich_thu vat , tien truc_tiep lien_quan den toi_pham
1 . Viec tich_thu , sung quy nha_nuoc duoc ap_dung doi_voi :
a ) Cong_cu , phuong_tien dung vao viec pham_toi ;
b ) Vat hoac tien_do pham_toi hoac do mua_ban , doi_chac nhung thu ay ma_co ;
c ) Vat thuoc loai Nha_nuoc cam luu_hanh .
2 . Doi_voi vat , tien bi nguoi pham_toi chiem_doat hoac su_dung trai_phep , thi khong tich_thu ma tra lai cho chu_so_huu hoac nguoi_quan_ly hop_phap .
3 . Vat , tien thuoc tai_san cua nguoi khac , neu nguoi nay co loi trong viec de cho_nguoi pham_toi su_dung vao viec thuc_hien toi_pham , thi co_the bi tich_thu , sung quy nha_nuoc .
Dieu 42 . Tra lai tai_san , sua_chua hoac boi_thuong thiet_hai ; buoc cong_khai xin_loi
1 . Nguoi pham_toi phai tra lai tai_san da chiem_doat cho chu_so_huu hoac nguoi_quan_ly hop_phap , phai sua_chua hoac boi_thuong thiet_hai vat_chat da duoc xac_dinh do hanh_vi pham_toi gay ra .
2 . Trong truong_hop pham_toi gay thiet_hai ve tinh_than , Toa_an buoc nguoi pham_toi phai boi_thuong ve vat_chat , cong_khai xin_loi nguoi_bi_hai .
Dieu 43 . Bat_buoc chua benh
1 . Doi_voi nguoi thuc_hien hanh_vi nguy_hiem cho xa_hoi trong khi mac benh quy_dinh tai_khoan 1 Dieu 13 cua Bo_luat nay , thi tuy theo giai_doan to_tung , Vien_kiem_sat hoac Toa_an can_cu vao ket_luan cua Hoi_dong giam_dinh phap_y , co_the quyet_dinh dua ho vao mot co_so dieu_tri chuyen_khoa de_bat buoc chua benh ; neu thay khong can_thiet phai dua vao mot co_so dieu_tri chuyen_khoa , thi co_the giao cho gia_dinh hoac nguoi giam_ho trong_nom duoi su giam_sat cua_co quan_nha nuoc co tham_quyen .
2 . Doi_voi nguoi pham_toi trong khi co_nang luc trach_nhiem hinh_su nhung truoc khi bi ket_an da mac benh toi muc mat kha_nang nhan_thuc hoac kha_nang dieu_khien hanh_vi cua_minh , thi can_cu vao ket_luan cua Hoi_dong giam_dinh phap_y , Toa_an co_the quyet_dinh dua ho vao mot co_so dieu_tri chuyen_khoa de_bat buoc chua benh . Sau khi khoi benh , nguoi do co_the phai chiu trach_nhiem hinh_su .
3 . Doi_voi nguoi dang chap_hanh hinh_phat ma bi benh toi muc mat kha_nang nhan_thuc hoac kha_nang dieu_khien hanh_vi cua_minh , thi can_cu vao ket_luan cua Hoi_dong giam_dinh phap_y , Toa_an co_the quyet_dinh dua ho vao mot co_so dieu_tri chuyen_khoa de_bat buoc chua benh . Sau khi khoi benh , nguoi do phai tiep_tuc chap_hanh hinh_phat , neu khong co ly_do khac de mien chap_hanh hinh_phat .
Dieu 44 . Thoi_gian bat_buoc chua benh
Can_cu vao ket_luan cua co_so dieu_tri , neu nguoi bi bat_buoc chua benh quy_dinh tai Dieu 43 cua Bo_luat nay da khoi benh , thi tuy theo giai_doan to_tung , Vien_kiem_sat hoac Toa_an xet va quyet_dinh dinh_chi viec thi_hanh bien_phap nay .
Thoi_gian bat_buoc chua benh duoc tru vao thoi_han chap_hanh hinh_phat tu .
Chuong VII
QUYET_DINH_HINH_PHAT
Dieu 45 . Can_cu quyet_dinh hinh_phat
Khi quyet_dinh hinh_phat , Toa_an can_cu vao quy_dinh cua Bo_luat hinh_su , can_nhac tinh_chat va muc_do nguy_hiem cho xa_hoi cua hanh_vi pham_toi , nhan_than nguoi pham_toi , cac tinh_tiet giam nhe va tang nang trach_nhiem hinh_su .
Dieu 46 . Cac tinh_tiet giam nhe trach_nhiem hinh_su
1 . Cac tinh_tiet sau day la tinh_tiet giam nhe trach_nhiem hinh_su :
a ) Nguoi pham toi_da ngan_chan , lam giam bot tac_hai cua toi_pham ;
b ) Nguoi pham_toi tu_nguyen sua_chua , boi_thuong thiet_hai , khac_phuc hau_qua ;
c ) Pham_toi trong truong_hop vuot qua gioi_han phong_ve chinh_dang ;
d ) Pham_toi trong truong_hop vuot qua yeu_cau cua tinh_the cap_thiet ;
d ) Pham_toi trong truong_hop bi kich_dong ve tinh_than do hanh_vi trai phap_luat cua nguoi_bi_hai hoac nguoi khac gay ra ;
e ) Pham_toi vi hoan_canh dac_biet kho_khan ma khong phai do minh tu gay ra ;
g ) Pham_toi nhung chua gay thiet_hai hoac gay thiet_hai khong lon ;
h ) Pham_toi lan dau va thuoc truong_hop it nghiem_trong ;
i ) Pham_toi vi bi nguoi khac de_doa , cuong_buc ;
k ) Pham_toi do lac_hau ;
l ) Nguoi pham_toi la phu_nu co_thai ;
m ) Nguoi pham_toi la nguoi gia ;
n ) Nguoi pham_toi la nguoi co benh bi han_che kha_nang nhan_thuc hoac kha_nang dieu_khien hanh_vi cua_minh ;
o ) Nguoi pham_toi tu_thu ;
p ) Nguoi pham_toi thanh_khan khai_bao , an_nan hoi_cai ;
q ) Nguoi pham_toi tich_cuc giup_do cac co_quan co trach_nhiem phat_hien , dieu_tra toi_pham ;
r ) Nguoi pham toi_da lap_cong chuoc toi ;
s ) Nguoi pham_toi la nguoi co thanh_tich xuat_sac trong san_xuat , chien_dau , hoc_tap hoac cong_tac .
2 . Khi quyet_dinh hinh_phat , Toa_an con co_the coi cac tinh_tiet khac la tinh_tiet giam nhe , nhung phai ghi ro trong_ban an .
3 . Cac tinh_tiet giam nhe_da duoc Bo_luat hinh_su quy_dinh la dau_hieu dinh_toi hoac dinh khung thi khong duoc coi la tinh_tiet giam nhe trong khi quyet_dinh hinh_phat .
Dieu 47 . Quyet_dinh hinh_phat nhe hon quy_dinh cua Bo_luat
Khi co it_nhat hai tinh_tiet giam nhe quy_dinh tai_khoan 1 Dieu 46 cua Bo_luat nay , Toa_an co_the quyet_dinh mot hinh_phat duoi muc thap nhat cua khung_hinh_phat ma dieu_luat da quy_dinh nhung phai trong khung_hinh_phat lien ke_nhe hon cua dieu_luat ; trong truong_hop dieu_luat chi co mot khung_hinh_phat hoac khung_hinh_phat do_la khung_hinh_phat nhe nhat cua dieu_luat , thi Toa_an co_the quyet_dinh mot hinh_phat duoi muc thap nhat cua khung hoac chuyen sang mot hinh_phat khac thuoc loai nhe hon . Ly_do cua viec giam nhe phai duoc ghi ro trong_ban an .
Dieu 48 . Cac tinh_tiet tang nang trach_nhiem hinh_su
1 . Chi cac tinh_tiet sau day moi_la tinh_tiet tang nang trach_nhiem hinh_su :
a ) Pham_toi co to_chuc ;
b ) Pham_toi co_tinh chat chuyen_nghiep ;
c ) Loi_dung chuc_vu , quyen_han de pham_toi ;
d ) Pham_toi co_tinh chat con_do ;
d ) Pham_toi vi dong_co de_hen ;
e ) Co_tinh thuc_hien toi_pham den_cung ;
g ) Pham_toi nhieu lan , tai_pham , tai_pham nguy_hiem ;
h ) Pham_toi doi_voi tre_em , phu_nu co_thai , nguoi gia , nguoi o trong tinh_trang khong_the tu_ve duoc hoac doi_voi nguoi le_thuoc minh ve mat vat_chat , tinh_than , cong_tac hoac cac mat_khac ;
i ) Xam_pham tai_san cua Nha_nuoc ;
k ) Pham_toi gay hau_qua nghiem_trong , rat nghiem_trong hoac dac_biet nghiem_trong ;
l ) Loi_dung hoan_canh chien_tranh , tinh_trang khan_cap , thien_tai , dich_benh hoac nhung kho_khan dac_biet khac cua xa_hoi de pham_toi ;
m ) Dung thu_doan xao_quyet , tan_ac pham_toi hoac thu_doan , phuong_tien co kha_nang gay nguy_hai cho nhieu nguoi ;
n ) Xui_giuc nguoi chua thanh_nien pham_toi ;
o ) Co hanh_dong xao_quyet , hung_han nham tron_tranh , che_giau toi_pham .
2 . Nhung tinh_tiet da la yeu_to dinh_toi hoac dinh khung_hinh_phat thi khong duoc coi la tinh_tiet tang nang .
Dieu 49 . Tai_pham , tai_pham nguy_hiem
1 . Tai_pham la truong_hop da bi ket_an , chua duoc xoa an tich ma_lai pham_toi do co_y hoac pham_toi rat nghiem_trong , toi dac_biet nghiem_trong do_vo y .
2 . Nhung truong_hop sau day duoc coi la tai_pham nguy_hiem :
a ) Da bi ket_an ve toi rat nghiem_trong , toi dac_biet nghiem_trong do co_y , chua duoc xoa an tich ma_lai pham_toi rat nghiem_trong , toi dac_biet nghiem_trong do co_y ;
b ) Da tai_pham , chua duoc xoa an tich ma_lai pham_toi do co_y .
Dieu 50 . Quyet_dinh hinh_phat trong truong_hop pham nhieu toi
Khi xet_xu cung mot lan mot nguoi pham nhieu toi , Toa_an quyet_dinh hinh_phat doi_voi tung toi , sau_do tong_hop hinh_phat theo quy_dinh sau day :
1 . Doi_voi hinh_phat chinh :
a ) Neu cac hinh_phat da tuyen cung la cai_tao khong giam_giu hoac cung la tu co thoi_han , thi cac hinh_phat do_duoc cong lai thanh hinh_phat chung ; hinh_phat chung khong duoc vuot qua ba nam doi_voi hinh_phat cai_tao khong giam_giu , ba muoi nam doi_voi hinh_phat tu co thoi_han ;
b ) Neu cac hinh_phat da tuyen la cai_tao khong giam_giu , tu co thoi_han , thi hinh_phat cai_tao khong giam_giu duoc chuyen_doi thanh hinh_phat tu theo ty_le cu ba ngay cai_tao khong giam_giu duoc chuyen_doi thanh mot ngay tu de tong_hop thanh hinh_phat chung theo quy_dinh tai diem a khoan 1 Dieu_nay ;
c ) Neu hinh_phat nang nhat trong so cac hinh_phat da tuyen la tu chung_than thi hinh_phat chung la tu chung_than ;
d ) Neu hinh_phat nang nhat trong so cac hinh_phat da tuyen la tu_hinh thi hinh_phat chung la tu_hinh ;
d ) Phat tien khong tong_hop voi cac loai hinh_phat khac ; cac khoan tien phat duoc cong lai thanh hinh_phat chung ;
e ) Truc_xuat khong tong_hop voi cac loai hinh_phat khac .
2 . Doi_voi hinh_phat bo_sung :
a ) Neu cac hinh_phat da tuyen la cung loai thi hinh_phat chung duoc quyet_dinh trong gioi_han do Bo_luat nay quy_dinh doi_voi loai hinh_phat do ; rieng doi_voi hinh_phat tien thi cac khoan tien phat duoc cong lai thanh hinh_phat chung ;
b ) Neu cac hinh_phat da tuyen la khac loai thi nguoi bi ket_an phai chap_hanh tat_ca cac hinh_phat da tuyen .
Dieu 51 . Tong_hop hinh_phat cua nhieu ban_an
1 . Trong truong_hop mot nguoi dang_phai chap_hanh mot ban_an ma_lai bi xet_xu ve toi_da pham truoc khi co_ban an nay , thi Toa_an quyet_dinh hinh_phat doi_voi toi dang bi xet_xu , sau_do quyet_dinh hinh_phat chung theo quy_dinh tai Dieu 50 cua Bo_luat nay .
Thoi_gian da chap_hanh hinh_phat cua ban_an truoc duoc tru vao thoi_han chap_hanh hinh_phat chung .
2 . Khi xet_xu mot nguoi dang_phai chap_hanh mot ban_an ma_lai pham_toi moi , Toa_an quyet_dinh hinh_phat doi_voi toi moi , sau_do tong_hop voi phan hinh_phat chua chap_hanh cua ban_an truoc roi quyet_dinh hinh_phat chung theo quy_dinh tai Dieu 50 cua Bo_luat nay .
3 . Trong truong_hop mot nguoi phai chap_hanh nhieu ban_an da_co hieu_luc phap_luat ma cac hinh_phat cua cac_ban an_chua duoc tong_hop , thi Chanh_an Toa_an ra quyet_dinh tong_hop cac_ban an_theo quy_dinh tai_khoan 1 va khoan 2 Dieu_nay .
Dieu 52 . Quyet_dinh hinh_phat trong truong_hop chuan_bi pham_toi , pham_toi chua_dat
1 . Doi_voi hanh_vi chuan_bi pham_toi va hanh_vi pham_toi chua_dat , hinh_phat duoc quyet_dinh theo cac dieu cua Bo_luat nay ve cac toi_pham tuong_ung tuy theo tinh_chat , muc_do nguy_hiem cho xa_hoi cua hanh_vi , muc_do thuc_hien y_dinh pham_toi va nhung tinh_tiet khac khien cho toi_pham khong thuc_hien duoc den_cung .
2 . Doi_voi truong_hop chuan_bi pham_toi , neu dieu_luat duoc ap_dung co quy_dinh hinh_phat cao nhat_la tu chung_than hoac tu_hinh , thi muc hinh_phat cao nhat duoc ap_dung la khong qua hai muoi nam tu ; neu la tu co thoi_han thi muc hinh_phat khong qua mot phan hai muc phat tu ma dieu_luat quy_dinh .
3 . Doi_voi truong_hop pham_toi chua_dat , neu dieu_luat duoc ap_dung co quy_dinh hinh_phat cao nhat_la tu chung_than hoac tu_hinh , thi chi co_the ap_dung cac hinh_phat nay trong truong_hop dac_biet nghiem_trong ; neu la tu co thoi_han thi muc hinh_phat khong qua ba phan_tu muc phat tu ma dieu_luat quy_dinh .
Dieu 53 . Quyet_dinh hinh_phat trong truong_hop dong_pham
Khi quyet_dinh hinh_phat doi_voi nhung nguoi dong_pham , Toa_an phai xet den tinh_chat cua dong_pham , tinh_chat va muc_do tham_gia pham_toi cua tung nguoi dong_pham .
Cac tinh_tiet giam nhe , tang nang hoac loai_tru trach_nhiem hinh_su thuoc nguoi dong_pham nao , thi chi ap_dung doi_voi nguoi do .
Dieu 54 . Mien hinh_phat
Nguoi pham_toi co_the duoc mien hinh_phat trong truong_hop pham_toi co nhieu tinh_tiet giam nhe quy_dinh tai_khoan 1 Dieu 46 cua Bo_luat nay , dang duoc khoan_hong dac_biet , nhung chua den muc duoc mien trach_nhiem hinh_su .
Chuong VIII
THOI_HIEU_THI_HANH_BAN_AN , MIEN_CHAP_HANH_HINH_PHAT , GIAM_THOI_HAN_CHAP_HANH_HINH_PHAT
Dieu 55 . Thoi_hieu thi_hanh ban_an
1 . Thoi_hieu thi_hanh ban_an hinh_su la thoi_han do Bo_luat nay quy_dinh ma khi het thoi_han do_nguoi bi ket_an khong phai chap_hanh ban_an da tuyen .
2 . Thoi_hieu thi_hanh ban_an hinh_su duoc quy_dinh nhu sau :
a ) Nam nam doi_voi cac truong_hop xu_phat tien , cai_tao khong giam_giu hoac xu_phat tu tu_ba nam tro xuong ;
b ) Muoi nam doi_voi cac truong_hop xu_phat tu_tu tren ba nam den muoi lam nam ;
c ) Muoi lam nam doi_voi cac truong_hop xu_phat tu_tu tren muoi lam nam den ba muoi nam .
3 . Thoi_hieu thi_hanh ban_an hinh_su duoc_tinh tu ngay ban_an co_hieu luc phap_luat . Neu trong thoi_han quy_dinh tai_khoan 2 Dieu_nay nguoi bi ket_an lai pham_toi moi , thi thoi_gian da qua khong duoc_tinh va thoi_hieu tinh lai ke tu ngay pham_toi moi .
Neu trong thoi_han quy_dinh tai_khoan 2 Dieu_nay nguoi bi ket_an co_tinh tron_tranh va da_co lenh truy_na , thi thoi_gian tron_tranh khong duoc_tinh va thoi_hieu tinh lai ke tu ngay nguoi do ra trinh_dien hoac bi bat_giu .
4 . Viec ap_dung thoi_hieu doi_voi cac truong_hop xu_phat tu chung_than hoac tu_hinh , sau khi da qua thoi_han muoi lam nam , do Chanh_an Toa_an nhan_dan toi_cao quyet_dinh theo de_nghi cua Vien_truong Vien_kiem_sat nhan_dan toi_cao . Trong truong_hop khong cho ap_dung thoi_hieu thi hinh_phat tu_hinh duoc chuyen thanh_tu chung_than , tu chung_than duoc chuyen thanh tu_ba muoi nam .
Dieu 56 . Khong ap_dung thoi_hieu thi_hanh ban_an
Khong ap_dung thoi_hieu thi_hanh ban_an doi_voi cac toi quy_dinh tai Chuong XI va Chuong XIV cua Bo_luat nay .
Dieu 57 . Mien chap_hanh hinh_phat
1 . Doi_voi nguoi bi ket_an cai_tao khong giam_giu , tu co thoi_han , chua chap_hanh hinh_phat ma lap_cong lon hoac mac benh hiem_ngheo va neu nguoi do khong con nguy_hiem cho xa_hoi nua , thi theo de_nghi cua Vien_truong Vien_Kiem sat , Toa_an co_the quyet_dinh mien chap_hanh toan_bo hinh_phat .
2 . Nguoi bi ket_an duoc mien chap_hanh hinh_phat khi duoc dac_xa hoac dai_xa .
3 . Doi_voi nguoi bi ket_an ve toi it nghiem_trong da duoc hoan chap_hanh hinh_phat theo quy_dinh tai Dieu 61 cua Bo_luat nay , neu trong thoi_gian duoc hoan da lap_cong , thi theo de_nghi cua Vien_truong Vien_Kiem sat , Toa_an co_the quyet_dinh mien chap_hanh hinh_phat .
4 . Doi_voi nguoi bi ket_an phat tu_ve toi it nghiem_trong da duoc tam dinh_chi chap_hanh hinh_phat theo quy_dinh tai Dieu 62 cua Bo_luat nay , neu trong thoi_gian duoc tam dinh_chi ma da lap_cong , thi theo de_nghi cua Vien_truong Vien_Kiem sat , Toa_an co_the quyet_dinh mien chap_hanh phan hinh_phat con lai .
5 . Nguoi bi phat cam cu_tru hoac quan che , neu da chap_hanh duoc mot phan hai thoi_han hinh phat_va cai_tao tot , thi theo de_nghi cua chinh_quyen dia_phuong noi nguoi do chap_hanh hinh_phat , Toa_an co_the quyet_dinh mien chap_hanh phan hinh_phat con lai .
Dieu 58 . Giam_muc hinh_phat da tuyen
1 . Nguoi bi ket_an cai_tao khong giam_giu , neu da chap_hanh hinh_phat duoc mot thoi_gian nhat_dinh va co nhieu tien_bo , thi theo de_nghi cua co_quan , to_chuc hoac chinh_quyen dia_phuong duoc giao trach_nhiem truc_tiep giam_sat , giao_duc , Toa_an co_the quyet_dinh giam thoi_han chap_hanh hinh_phat .
Nguoi bi ket_an phat tu , neu da chap_hanh hinh_phat duoc mot thoi_gian nhat_dinh va co nhieu tien_bo , thi theo de_nghi cua co_quan thi_hanh an phat tu , Toa_an co_the quyet_dinh giam thoi_han chap_hanh hinh_phat .
Thoi_gian da chap_hanh hinh_phat de duoc xet giam lan dau la mot phan ba thoi_han doi_voi hinh_phat cai_tao khong giam_giu , hinh_phat tu tu_ba muoi nam tro xuong , muoi hai nam doi_voi tu chung_than .
2 . Nguoi bi ket_an phat tien da tich_cuc chap_hanh duoc mot phan hinh_phat nhung bi lam vao hoan_canh kinh_te dac_biet kho_khan keo dai do thien_tai , hoa_hoan , tai_nan hoac om_dau gay ra ma khong_the tiep_tuc chap_hanh duoc phan hinh_phat con lai hoac lap_cong lon , thi theo de_nghi cua Vien_truong Vien_kiem_sat , Toa_an co_the quyet_dinh mien viec chap_hanh phan tien phat con lai .
3 . Mot nguoi co_the duoc giam nhieu lan , nhung phai bao_dam chap_hanh duoc mot phan hai muc hinh_phat da tuyen . Nguoi bi ket_an tu chung_than , lan dau duoc giam xuong ba muoi nam tu_va du duoc giam nhieu lan cung phai bao_dam thoi_han thuc_te chap_hanh hinh_phat la hai muoi nam .
4 . Doi_voi nguoi da duoc giam mot phan hinh_phat ma_lai pham_toi moi nghiem_trong , rat nghiem_trong hoac dac_biet nghiem_trong , thi Toa_an chi xet giam lan dau sau khi nguoi do da chap_hanh duoc hai_phan ba muc hinh_phat chung hoac hai muoi nam neu la tu chung_than .
Dieu 59 . Giam thoi_han chap_hanh hinh_phat trong truong_hop dac_biet
Nguoi bi ket_an co_ly do_dang duoc khoan_hong them nhu_da lap_cong , da qua gia_yeu hoac mac benh hiem_ngheo , thi Toa_an co_the xet giam vao thoi_gian som hon hoac voi muc cao hon_so voi thoi_gian va muc quy_dinh tai Dieu 58 cua Bo_luat nay .
Dieu 60 . An_treo
1 . Khi xu_phat tu khong qua ba nam , can_cu vao nhan_than cua nguoi pham_toi va cac tinh_tiet giam nhe , neu xet thay khong can phai bat chap_hanh hinh_phat tu , thi Toa_an cho huong an_treo va an_dinh thoi_gian thu_thach tu mot nam den nam nam .
2 . Trong thoi_gian thu_thach , Toa_an giao nguoi duoc huong an_treo cho co_quan , to_chuc noi nguoi do lam_viec hoac chinh_quyen dia_phuong noi nguoi do thuong_tru de giam_sat va giao_duc . Gia_dinh nguoi bi ket_an co trach_nhiem phoi_hop voi co_quan , to_chuc , chinh_quyen dia_phuong trong viec giam_sat , giao_duc nguoi do .
3 . Nguoi duoc huong an_treo co_the phai chiu hinh_phat bo_sung la phat tien , cam dam_nhiem chuc_vu , cam hanh_nghe hoac lam cong_viec nhat_dinh theo quy_dinh tai Dieu 30 va Dieu 36 cua Bo_luat nay .
4 . Nguoi duoc huong an_treo da chap_hanh duoc mot phan hai thoi_gian thu_thach va co nhieu tien_bo thi theo de_nghi cua co_quan , to_chuc co trach_nhiem giam_sat va giao_duc , Toa_an co_the quyet_dinh rut ngan thoi_gian thu_thach .
5 . Doi_voi nguoi duoc huong an_treo ma pham_toi moi trong thoi_gian thu_thach , thi Toa_an quyet_dinh buoc phai chap_hanh hinh_phat cua ban_an truoc va tong_hop voi hinh_phat cua ban_an moi theo quy_dinh tai Dieu 51 cua Bo_luat nay .
Dieu 61 . Hoan chap_hanh hinh_phat tu
1 . Nguoi bi xu_phat tu co_the duoc hoan chap_hanh hinh_phat trong cac truong_hop sau day :
a ) Bi benh nang duoc hoan cho_den khi suc_khoe duoc hoi_phuc ;
b ) Phu_nu co_thai hoac dang nuoi con duoi 36 thang tuoi , thi duoc hoan cho_den khi con du 36 thang tuoi ;
c ) La nguoi lao_dong duy_nhat trong gia_dinh , neu phai chap_hanh hinh_phat tu_thi gia_dinh se gap kho_khan dac_biet , duoc hoan den mot nam , tru truong_hop nguoi do bi ket_an ve cac toi xam_pham an_ninh quoc_gia hoac cac toi khac la toi rat nghiem_trong , dac_biet nghiem_trong ;
d ) Bi ket_an ve toi it nghiem_trong , do_nhu cau_cong vu , thi duoc hoan den mot nam .
2 . Trong thoi_gian duoc hoan chap_hanh hinh_phat tu , neu nguoi duoc hoan chap_hanh hinh_phat lai pham_toi moi , thi Toa_an buoc nguoi do phai chap_hanh hinh_phat truoc va tong_hop voi hinh_phat cua ban_an moi theo quy_dinh tai Dieu 51 cua Bo_luat nay .
Dieu 62 . Tam dinh_chi chap_hanh hinh_phat tu
1 . Nguoi dang chap_hanh hinh_phat tu ma thuoc mot trong cac truong_hop quy_dinh tai_khoan 1 Dieu 61 cua Bo_luat nay , thi co_the duoc tam dinh_chi chap_hanh hinh_phat tu .
2 . Thoi_gian tam dinh_chi khong duoc_tinh vao thoi_gian chap_hanh hinh_phat tu .
Chuong IX
XOA_AN_TICH
Dieu 63 . Xoa an tich
Nguoi bi ket_an duoc xoa an tich theo quy_dinh tai cac dieu tu Dieu 64 den Dieu 67 cua Bo_luat nay .
Nguoi duoc xoa an tich coi nhu chua bi ket_an va duoc Toa_an cap giay chung_nhan .
Dieu 64 . Duong_nhien duoc xoa an tich
Nhung nguoi sau day duong_nhien duoc xoa an tich :
1 . Nguoi duoc mien hinh_phat .
2 . Nguoi bi ket_an khong phai ve cac toi quy_dinh tai Chuong XI va Chuong XIV cua Bo_luat nay , neu tu khi chap_hanh xong ban_an hoac tu khi het thoi_hieu thi_hanh ban_an nguoi do khong pham_toi moi trong thoi_han sau day :
a ) Mot nam trong truong_hop bi phat canh_cao , phat tien , cai_tao khong giam_giu hoac phat tu nhung duoc huong an_treo ;
b ) Ba nam trong_trong truong_hop hinh_phat la tu den ba nam ;
c ) Nam nam trong truong_hop hinh_phat la tu_tu tren ba nam den muoi lam nam ;
d ) Bay nam trong truong_hop hinh_phat la tu_tu tren muoi lam nam .
Dieu 65 . Xoa an tich theo quyet_dinh cua Toa_an
1 . Toa_an quyet_dinh viec xoa an tich doi_voi nhung nguoi da bi ket_an ve cac toi quy_dinh tai Chuong XI va Chuong XIV cua Bo_luat nay , can_cu vao tinh_chat cua toi_pham da duoc thuc_hien , nhan_than , thai_do chap_hanh phap_luat va thai_do lao_dong cua nguoi bi ket_an trong cac truong_hop sau day :
a ) Da bi phat tu den ba nam ma khong pham_toi moi trong thoi_han ba nam , ke tu khi chap_hanh xong ban_an hoac tu khi het thoi_hieu thi_hanh ban_an ;
b ) Da bi phat tu_tu tren ba nam den muoi lam nam ma khong pham_toi moi trong thoi_han bay nam , ke tu khi chap_hanh xong ban_an hoac tu khi het thoi_hieu thi_hanh ban_an ;
c ) Da bi phat tu tren muoi lam nam ma khong pham_toi moi trong thoi_han muoi nam , ke tu khi chap_hanh xong ban_an hoac tu khi het thoi_hieu thi_hanh ban_an .
2 . Nguoi bi Toa_an bac don xin xoa an tich lan dau phai cho mot nam sau moi duoc xin xoa an tich ; neu bi bac don lan thu_hai tro di thi_phai sau hai nam moi duoc xin xoa an tich .
Dieu 66 . Xoa an tich trong truong_hop dac_biet
Trong truong_hop nguoi bi ket_an co nhung bieu_hien tien_bo ro_ret va da lap_cong , duoc co_quan , to_chuc noi nguoi do cong_tac hoac chinh_quyen dia_phuong noi nguoi do thuong_tru de_nghi , thi co_the duoc Toa_an xoa an tich neu nguoi do da bao_dam duoc it_nhat mot phan ba thoi_han quy_dinh .
Dieu 67 . Cach tinh thoi_han de xoa an tich
1 . Thoi_han de xoa an tich quy_dinh tai Dieu 64 va Dieu 65 cua Bo_luat nay can_cu vao hinh_phat chinh da tuyen .
2 . Neu chua duoc xoa an tich ma pham_toi moi , thi thoi_han de xoa an tich cu tinh_tu ngay chap_hanh xong ban_an moi .
3 . Viec chap_hanh xong ban_an bao_gom viec chap_hanh xong hinh_phat chinh , hinh_phat bo_sung va cac quyet_dinh khac cua ban_an .
4 . Nguoi duoc mien chap_hanh phan hinh_phat con lai cung duoc coi nhu_da chap_hanh xong hinh_phat .
Chuong X
NHUNG_QUY_DINH_DOI_VOI_NGUOI_CHUA_THANH_NIEN_PHAM_TOI
Dieu 68 . Ap_dung Bo_luat hinh_su doi_voi nguoi chua thanh_nien pham_toi
Nguoi chua thanh_nien tu du 14 tuoi den duoi 18 tuoi pham_toi phai chiu trach_nhiem hinh_su theo nhung quy_dinh cua Chuong nay , dong_thoi theo nhung quy_dinh khac cua Phan_chung Bo_luat khong trai voi nhung quy_dinh cua Chuong nay .
Dieu 69 . Nguyen_tac xu_ly doi_voi nguoi chua thanh_nien pham_toi
1 . Viec xu_ly nguoi chua thanh_nien pham_toi chu_yeu nham giao_duc , giup do_ho sua_chua sai_lam , phat_trien lanh_manh va tro_thanh cong_dan co_ich cho xa_hoi .
Trong moi_truong hop dieu_tra , truy_to , xet_xu hanh_vi pham_toi cua nguoi chua thanh_nien , cac_co quan_nha nuoc co tham_quyen phai xac_dinh kha_nang nhan_thuc cua ho_ve tinh_chat nguy_hiem cho xa_hoi cua hanh_vi pham_toi , nguyen_nhan va dieu_kien gay ra toi_pham .
2 . Nguoi chua thanh_nien pham_toi co_the duoc mien trach_nhiem hinh_su , neu nguoi do pham_toi it nghiem_trong hoac toi nghiem_trong , gay hai khong lon , co nhieu tinh_tiet giam nhe va duoc gia_dinh hoac co_quan , to_chuc nhan giam_sat , giao_duc .
3 . Viec truy_cuu trach_nhiem hinh_su nguoi chua thanh_nien pham_toi va ap_dung hinh_phat doi_voi ho duoc thuc_hien chi trong truong_hop can_thiet va phai can_cu vao tinh_chat cua hanh_vi pham_toi , vao nhung dac_diem ve nhan_than va yeu_cau cua viec phong_ngua toi_pham .
4 . Khi xet_xu , neu thay khong can_thiet phai ap_dung hinh_phat doi_voi nguoi chua thanh_nien pham_toi , thi Toa_an ap_dung mot trong cac bien_phap tu_phap duoc quy_dinh tai Dieu 70 cua Bo_luat nay .
5 . Khong xu_phat tu chung_than hoac tu_hinh doi_voi nguoi chua thanh_nien pham_toi . Khi xu_phat tu co thoi_han , Toa_an cho_nguoi chua thanh_nien pham_toi duoc huong muc an nhe hon muc an ap_dung doi_voi nguoi da thanh_nien pham_toi tuong_ung .
Khong ap_dung hinh_phat tien doi_voi nguoi chua thanh_nien pham_toi o_do tuoi tu du 14 tuoi den duoi 16 tuoi .
Khong ap_dung hinh_phat bo_sung doi_voi nguoi chua thanh_nien pham_toi .
6 . an da tuyen doi_voi nguoi chua thanh_nien pham_toi khi chua du 16 tuoi , thi khong tinh de xac_dinh tai_pham hoac tai_pham nguy_hiem .
Dieu 70 . Cac bien_phap tu_phap ap_dung doi_voi nguoi chua thanh_nien pham_toi
1 . Doi_voi nguoi chua thanh_nien pham_toi , Toa_an co_the quyet_dinh ap_dung mot trong cac bien_phap tu_phap co_tinh giao_duc , phong_ngua sau day :
a ) Giao_duc tai xa , phuong , thi_tran ;
b ) Dua vao truong_giao_duong .
2 . Toa_an co_the ap_dung bien_phap giao_duc tai xa , phuong , thi_tran tu mot nam den hai nam doi_voi nguoi chua thanh_nien pham_toi it nghiem_trong hoac toi nghiem_trong .
Nguoi duoc giao_duc tai xa , phuong , thi_tran phai chap_hanh day_du nhung nghia_vu ve hoc_tap , lao_dong , tuan theo phap_luat duoi su giam_sat , giao_duc cua chinh_quyen xa , phuong , thi_tran va to_chuc xa_hoi duoc Toa_an giao trach_nhiem .
3 . Toa_an co_the ap_dung bien_phap dua vao truong_giao_duong tu mot nam den hai nam doi_voi nguoi chua thanh_nien pham_toi , neu thay do tinh_chat nghiem_trong cua hanh_vi pham_toi , do nhan_than va moi_truong song cua nguoi do ma_can dua nguoi do vao mot to_chuc giao_duc co ky_luat chat_che .
4 . Neu nguoi duoc giao_duc tai xa , phuong , thi_tran hoac nguoi duoc dua vao truong_giao_duong da chap_hanh mot phan hai thoi_han do Toa_an quyet_dinh va co nhieu tien_bo , thi theo de_nghi cua to_chuc , co_quan , nha_truong duoc giao trach_nhiem giam_sat , giao_duc , Toa_an co_the quyet_dinh cham_dut thoi_han giao_duc tai xa , phuong , thi_tran hoac thoi_han o_truong giao_duong .
Dieu 71 . Cac hinh_phat duoc ap_dung doi_voi nguoi chua thanh_nien pham_toi
Nguoi chua thanh_nien pham_toi chi bi ap_dung mot trong cac hinh_phat sau day doi_voi moi toi_pham :
1 . Canh_cao ;
2 . Phat tien ;
3 . Cai_tao khong giam_giu ;
4 . Tu co thoi_han .
Dieu 72 . Phat tien
Phat tien duoc ap_dung la hinh_phat chinh doi_voi nguoi chua thanh_nien pham_toi tu du 16 tuoi den duoi 18 tuoi , neu nguoi do co thu_nhap hoac co tai_san rieng .
Muc phat tien doi_voi nguoi chua thanh_nien pham_toi khong qua mot phan hai muc tien phat ma dieu_luat quy_dinh .
Dieu 73 . Cai_tao khong giam_giu
Khi ap_dung hinh_phat cai_tao khong giam_giu doi_voi nguoi chua thanh_nien pham_toi , thi khong khau_tru thu_nhap cua nguoi do .
Thoi_han cai_tao khong giam_giu doi_voi nguoi chua thanh_nien pham_toi khong qua mot phan hai thoi_han ma dieu_luat quy_dinh .
Dieu 74 . Tu co thoi_han
Nguoi chua thanh_nien pham_toi chi bi phat tu co thoi_han theo quy_dinh sau day :
1 . Doi_voi nguoi tu du 16 tuoi den duoi 18 tuoi khi pham_toi , neu dieu_luat duoc ap_dung quy_dinh hinh_phat tu chung_than hoac tu_hinh , thi muc hinh_phat cao nhat duoc ap_dung khong qua muoi tam nam tu ; neu la tu co thoi_han thi muc hinh_phat cao nhat duoc ap_dung khong qua ba phan_tu muc phat tu ma dieu_luat quy_dinh ;
2 . Doi_voi nguoi tu du 14 tuoi den duoi 16 tuoi khi pham_toi , neu dieu_luat duoc ap_dung quy_dinh hinh_phat tu chung_than hoac tu_hinh , thi muc hinh_phat cao nhat duoc ap_dung khong qua muoi hai nam tu ; neu la tu co thoi_han thi muc hinh_phat cao nhat duoc ap_dung khong qua mot phan hai muc phat tu ma dieu_luat quy_dinh .
Dieu 75 . Tong_hop hinh_phat trong truong_hop pham nhieu toi
Doi_voi nguoi pham nhieu toi , co toi duoc thuc_hien truoc khi du 18 tuoi , co toi duoc thuc_hien sau khi du 18 tuoi , thi viec tong_hop hinh_phat ap_dung nhu sau :
1 . Neu toi nang nhat duoc thuc_hien khi nguoi do chua du 18 tuoi , thi hinh_phat chung khong duoc vuot qua muc hinh_phat cao nhat quy_dinh tai Dieu 74 cua Bo_luat nay ;
2 . Neu toi nang nhat duoc thuc_hien khi nguoi do da du 18 tuoi , thi hinh_phat chung ap_dung nhu doi_voi nguoi da thanh_nien pham_toi .
Dieu 76 . Giam_muc hinh_phat da tuyen
1 .
